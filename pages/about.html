About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at NUST in Zimbabwe, building systems to solve problems that actually exist not hypothetical ones, but real ones I see around me.

⚡

The Work

Nhimbe AI helps smallholder farmers access knowledge they've never had. Batana gives unbanked Zimbabweans a path to credit. PropVerifyZW fights property fraud. MineSafe keeps miners alive.

These aren't portfolio projects. They're attempts to move the needle on real world problems.

◎

Technical Foundation

I work with Python, JavaScript, SQL, React, Django, and Node.js. Comfortable picking up unfamiliar tools when a project needs them, managing multiple commitments, and working independently or in a team.

◈

The Mission

My ultimate goal is to build tailor-made solutions for African people. Systems large enough to power big-scale operations, systems small enough for a local trader to use.

"Scale doesn't move me. Impact does."
