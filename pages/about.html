About

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at NUST in Zimbabwe, building systems to solve problems that actually exist.

The Work

Not hypothetical problems in textbooks — real ones I see around me. Nhimbe AI helps smallholder farmers access knowledge they've never had. Batana gives unbanked Zimbabweans a path to credit and savings. PropVerifyZW fights property fraud that costs people life savings. MineSafe validates safety protocols that keep miners alive.

These aren't portfolio projects. They're attempts to move the needle on problems I care about.

Technical Foundation

I work with Python, JavaScript, SQL, React, Django, and Node.js. I'm comfortable picking up unfamiliar tools when a project needs them, managing multiple commitments without sacrificing quality, and working independently or as part of a team depending on what the work requires.

The Mission

My ultimate goal is to build tailor-made solutions for African people. Systems large enough to power big-scale operations, systems small enough for a local entrepreneur to use. Each one solving a real, tangible problem.

Scale doesn't move me. Impact does.
