PropVerifyZW

Verified Real Estate Platform

A verified digital property marketplace tackling fraud in Zimbabwe's real estate sector through Deeds Registry integration and licensed agent verification.

The Problem

Zimbabwe's real estate sector is plagued by fraudulent practices, lack of transparency, and unlicensed agents. Buyers, sellers, and tenants lose money through scams and fake listings - especially diaspora investors unfamiliar with the local landscape.

  • Fake listings and false ownership claims widespread
  • Many agents operate without proper accreditation
  • Buyers cannot verify property titles before committing funds
  • No centralized verified digital marketplace exists

Key Features

  • Tiered Verification - Government-verified via Deeds Registry
  • Verified Badge System for legitimate listings
  • Listings with maps, photos, and virtual tours
  • Secure escrow-based transactions
  • Agent reviews and transaction history
  • Advanced search by type, price, and categories
  • Resource hub - guides on rights and legal processes

Stakeholders

Government Ministry of Lands - Deeds Registry
Regulatory Real Estate Council of Zimbabwe
Financial Banks and mobile money operators
End Users Buyers, tenants, investors, agents

MineSafe

AI-Powered Mine Safety Detection System

Computer vision system using YOLOv8 to validate complete protective equipment at mine entrances. Automatically grants or denies access based on real-time PPE detection - eliminating human error entirely.

The Problem

  • Guards miss equipment or grow complacent during repetitive checks
  • Different inspectors apply inconsistent standards
  • Paper-based logs are unreliable with no audit trail
  • Manual checks create dangerous bottlenecks at shift changes
  • No accountability without reliable compliance records

How It Works

  • Camera at mine entrance captures each worker approaching
  • YOLOv8 analyzes the video feed in real-time
  • Detects presence or absence of 5 mandatory PPE items
  • Access automatically granted or denied based on result
  • Voice feedback announces the decision audibly
  • 3D visualization displays detection on a human figure
  • Every scan logged with timestamps and confidence scores
  • Admin override, monitoring, and report generation
