About

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at the National University of Science and Technology in Zimbabwe. My work so far has been academic, but the projects I build - Nhimbe AI, Batana, PropVerifyZW, MineSafe are attempts to solve real problems I see around me: farmers without accessible knowledge systems, people excluded from financial tools, unsafe mining operations, property fraud.

I having been work with Python, JavaScript, SQL, React, and Django. I'm comfortable picking up unfamiliar tools when a project needs them, managing multiple commitments without sacrificing quality, and working independently or as part of a team depending on what the work requires.
